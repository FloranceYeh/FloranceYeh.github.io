分享一些英文爆笑梗

纯文梗：

  • What do you call a deer with no eye?
    • No eye deer (No idea).
  • What did a late tomato say to other tomatoes?
    • I will ketchup (catch up). &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (ketchup 蕃茄酱)
  • I only know 25 letters of the alphabet.
    • why?
    • I don’t know Y (why).
  • What do you call a bee from America?
    • A USB (US bee).
  • Which is faster, hot or cold?
    • Hot. Because you can catch a cold. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (catch a cold 感冒)
  • Why is “dark” is spelled with a ‘k’ not a ‘c’? &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 单个字符要用单引号
    • Because you can’t c (see) in the dark.
  • Why shouldn’t we give Elsa a balloon?
    • Because she will Let It Go. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 迪士尼不会找我要版权吧
  • Who is closer to you, your mom or your dad?
    • Mom is close, because dad is farther.
