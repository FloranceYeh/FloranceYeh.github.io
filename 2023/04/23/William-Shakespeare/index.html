William Shakespeare

–Bard of Avon

我们都知道莎士比亚，但你觉得不知道这是一位多么伟大的诗人。
虽然莎士比亚以戏剧闻名，但我仍想称他为传奇诗人。

William Shakespeare, Shakespeare also spelled Shakspere, byname Bard of Avon or Swan of Avon,
(baptized April 26, 1564, Stratford-upon-Avon, Warwickshire, England—died April 23, 1616, Stratford-upon-Avon)，
English poet, dramatist, and actor often called the English national poet and considered by many to be

the greatest dramatist of all time.

  • To be or not to be: that is a question. (Hamlet 3.1)
    生存还是毁灭，这是个值得考虑的问题。——莎士比亚《哈姆雷特》

  • Brevity is the soul of wit. (Hamlet 2.2)
    简洁是智慧的灵魂，冗长是肤浅的藻饰。——莎士比亚《哈姆雷特》

  • Love is merely a madness. (As You Like It, 3.2)
    爱情不过是一种疯狂。——莎士比亚《皆大欢喜》

  • The course of true love never did run smooth. (A Midsummer Night’s Dream 1.1)
    真爱无坦途。 ——莎士比亚《仲夏夜之梦》

  • Keep up your bright swords, for the dew will rust them. (Othello 1.2)
    收起你们明晃晃的剑，它们沾了露水会生锈的。——莎士比亚《奥赛罗》

  • The lunatic, the lover and the poet are of imagination all compact. (A Midsummer Night’s Dream 5.1)
    疯子、情人、诗人都是想象的产儿。——莎士比亚《仲夏夜之梦》

  • Some rise by sin, and some by virtue fall. (Measure for Measure 2.1)
    有些人因罪恶而升迁，有些人因德行而没落。——莎士比亚《一报还一报》
